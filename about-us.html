Our Story

FlexJobs was founded with a singular mission: to revolutionize how professionals discover legitimate remote work opportunities. In a landscape saturated with scams and unreliable listings, we recognized a critical gap in the market. Our founders understood firsthand the frustration of job seekers navigating through countless fraudulent postings, wasting valuable time and energy on positions that simply don't exist.

What began as a small initiative to curate genuine remote job listings has evolved into the world's largest platform dedicated to remote work. Today, FlexJobs serves millions of job seekers worldwide, providing them with expertly vetted opportunities from reputable companies. We've processed over 100,000 job listings, screening each one meticulously to ensure authenticity and legitimacy. Our commitment to quality over quantity has established FlexJobs as the gold standard in remote job placement.

From our headquarters to our distributed team across the globe, everyone at FlexJobs shares a common passion: empowering individuals to find meaningful work on their own terms.
