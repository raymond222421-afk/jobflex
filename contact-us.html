Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about FlexJobs and our services

How long does it take to hear back?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 business hours. For urgent matters, please call our support line during business hours for immediate assistance.

Can companies post jobs on FlexJobs?

Yes! We partner with companies of all sizes. If you're interested in posting remote positions, please contact our partnership team at partnerships@flexjobs.com.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel anytime from your account settings with just a few clicks. We also offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee if you're not completely happy.

Is there a satisfaction guarantee?

Absolutely! We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you don't find suitable job opportunities within 30 days, we'll refund your full subscription fee.

How are jobs verified?

Our expert team manually reviews every job listing. We verify company information, authenticate job requirements, and ensure compliance with our quality standards.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. All transactions are secure and encrypted for your protection.
